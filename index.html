About RRHET

Raja Ratna Healthcare and Educational Trust with the objective of providing Technical Education par excellence to the Rural & Semi-urban students of East Godavari, West Godavari, Visakhapatnam and Srikakulam districts. The college was located at 5 Km from the distance of Ramachandrapuram town, and 20 km from Kakinada, Head quarter of E. G. Dt (A.P) the College has a serene Environment, ideal for effective teaching – learning process. It has sufficient infrastructure with an administration block of 24,000 sq. ft.

It facilitates AC Auditorium, AC Conference- room, well equipped laboratories, free internet with 70 Mbps leased line, a world class Library server with 2.5 tb back up area, an A.T.M. H.T. Power supply two generator sets of capacity 100 kva, excellent canteen to cater to the needs of students and staff.
